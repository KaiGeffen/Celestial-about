Explain this game

In Celestial TCG, every match is a story waiting to be told. Unlike traditional card games focused on combat, Celestial invites you to weave narratives through strategic card play, where your choices echo personal experiences and emotional journeys. Build your deck from a rotating collection of cards that represent life's moments - from quiet revelations to profound changes - and compete in a game where victory isn't just about power, but about crafting the most compelling tale. As day turns to night in each round, watch your story unfold before an audience, where every card played adds a new layer to your narrative. Whether you're exploring the single-player story mode or competing against others, Celestial offers a fresh take on card games where expression and strategy merge to create something truly unique.
