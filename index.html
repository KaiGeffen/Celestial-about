What is Celestial?

In this simple yet surprisingly deep card game, you build a deck of 15 from a rotating pool of cards, each representing themes and elements of our personal journeys.

Each match, you compete to win rounds through a distinctive day and night cycle: during the day, spend breath to play cards to a shared story, then at night, the cards resolve to determine the winner.

Whether you're exploring the single-player Journey mode or competing against other players online, Celestial offers a fresh take on card games where expression and strategy merge to create something truly unique.
